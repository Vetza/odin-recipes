Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

The greatest thing since sliced bread, this sandwich is best paired with a cold glass of milk. It is incredibly versatile as well, with honey being an amazing replacement for jam. I prefer mine lightly toasted with a dollop of peanut butter on the side as a dip!

Ingredients

  • Toasted White Bread
  • Strawberry Jam
  • Peanut Butter

Steps

  1. Grab your toasted bread and spread peanut butter generously across one side
  2. Spread strawberry jam on the other toast
  3. Put both sides together and enjoy!
