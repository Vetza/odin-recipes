Pizza

My all time favorite food, pizza is perfect for every occasion. Coupled with a good sauce and topped with a fine selection of ingredients, pizza outranks any other food by far!

ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Your choice of ingredients

Steps

  1. Spread your dough out in a circlular shape
  2. Spread tomato sauce all across the dough
  3. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella on top of the tomato sauce
  4. Add your choice of ingredients in any preferred combination over the cheese
  5. Place into the oven on high heat
  6. Cut into triangular pieces and enjoy!
